Library Policies

Membership

Free membership available to all community residents. Bring a valid ID and proof of address to register.

Borrowing Period

Books can be borrowed for up to 3 weeks with the option to renew twice if no holds are placed.

Late Returns

A grace period of 3 days is provided. After that, a nominal fee applies to encourage timely returns.

Conduct

Please maintain a respectful environment. Keep noise levels low and mobile devices on silent mode.
