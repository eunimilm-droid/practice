About the URSM Campus Library

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the services we provide.

Who We Are

The University of Rizal System Morong Campus Library is committed to serving students, faculty, researchers, and the local community by providing quality information resources and a supportive learning environment.

Our library stands as a hub for academic growth—promoting literacy, research, and continuous learning.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible and reliable information resources that empower students and faculty in achieving academic excellence and promoting lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic academic library that embraces innovation, technology, and collaborative learning, supporting the educational goals of the University of Rizal System.

Our Goals

• Strengthen access to diverse learning materials.
• Foster digital literacy through online resources.
• Support research development for all academic levels.
• Provide a safe and inclusive learning space.
